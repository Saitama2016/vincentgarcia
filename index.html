I strive to be a versatile developer by taking on the #100DaysofCode Twitter challenge to improve my understanding of server-side development, Linux, and Responsive Web Design for mobile apps. When I am not behind the computer screen, I am applying the Bulldog Mindset by going to tech-related networking events, working out at my best friend's garage, and hiking across the valleys.
